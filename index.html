Who am I?

Hello, my name is Bas a 28 year old guy from the Netherlands. From a young age I've been interested in computers and anything IT related. My interest in web development started back in 2007 when I made websites with a friend.

Fast-forward to today, I have 6 years experience as a product & UX/UI designer and switched to IT completely late 2020. My main focus these days is getting real good with HTML, CSS and JavaScript!

In the mean time I'm creating widgets, themes and more interesting things in my free time.
